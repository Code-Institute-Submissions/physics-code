Delimma Zone Calculator

Add your variables to the calculator - you will need to supply all the variables before being able to calculate whether or not a dilemma zone exists.

If a delimma zone exists, the output will show you the distance from the traffic light stop line and the size of the delimma zone.

The other possibility is that an option zone exists, this will be displayed as a distance from the traffic light stop line and the size of the option zone will also be given.
