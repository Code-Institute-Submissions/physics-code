Test Yourself

In this test you will be given one of four possible maps where you'll measure the intersection distance and be given random scenario for you to calculate if a dilemma zone or option zone exists. You can refer to our help by clicking on the info button at any time. Or consider using our calculator for best results.

For instructions on how to use Google Maps for this test click this button any time.

You may need to use some external web resources too for better understanding of variables such as reaction time and coefficient of friction.


Open the map

Use the map location provided by clicking on the map to answer questions about whether a dilemma zone exists or not.

Question One

Click on the map on or near the marker and make sure you're in satellite mode and once you have established road names you can turn off labels for ease in measuring the intersection.
