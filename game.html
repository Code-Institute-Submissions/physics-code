Reaction Time Game

If you're wanting to test your reaction time for the calculator, you can do so here by playing this game. The rules are very straight forward, but for the most accurate results, try and follow the rules as closely as possible.

Because we're dealing with reaction times between the pedals in a car, this test done by hand and mouse or touch screen may not give an accurate representation of reaction. This game is more for testing your reaction time as an estimate only.

Reaction times will vary with age, tiredness, distractions, impairment with alcohol or drugs and many other reasons.


Test your reaction time!

Click the "start" button and then move your hand at least 20 cm from the mouse. When you start, the lamp will turn green. When the lamp turns red, click the "Brake" button. Make sure you have not moved the mouse from the start. A timer will show how long it took for you to push the brakes.
